# Writing a blog, again

I decided, aided by claude code, to port my old blog on wordpress in a personal page here on github.

I think it is increasingly important to do my own amount of writing in the era of LLMs assisted writing.

I also love the idea of fully owning my content, and kind of hosting it, instead of using a social platform, with its heavy commercial influence to share my voice.

Also it is a good exercise into modern webpages design. I am using hugo after discussing with a LLM about the options i had available, for now i am sufficiently happy with the results. I think in the future maybe i could replicate a similar setup for different purposes, even if i would need AI support, given my lack of javascript expertise.

Anyway, i hope i will write more soon.
